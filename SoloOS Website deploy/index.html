The story

Built by someone who's been in your books.

I spent years managing the books for small businesses before I started running my own. The same problem showed up everywhere: most owners had no real system. Receipts in a folder, a spreadsheet that stopped making sense in March, and no clear picture of whether they were actually making money.

I built SoloOS because I couldn't find anything that worked for real people running real businesses. Not a course. Not a complicated accounting app. Just clean, fillable tools that give you the full picture without requiring a finance degree to use them.

Every product is designed to work on its own. They work even better together.

Questions

The ones we get most.

What format are the tools?
All SoloOS products are fillable PDFs. They work in Adobe Acrobat (free), GoodNotes, Notability, and most standard PDF readers. You can type directly into them on your computer or tablet, or print them and write by hand.
How do I get my purchase?
Instant digital download — your file is available immediately after purchase through Gumroad. You'll also receive a download link by email.
Can I use these on my phone?
Yes. Most PDF apps on iOS and Android support fillable forms. GoodNotes and Notability on iPad are especially popular for this. For the best experience, a computer or tablet is recommended.
Are these good for a new business or an established one?
Both. The Savings Challenge and Bill & Budget Tracker are great starting points if you're just getting organized. The CEO Dashboard and Side Hustle Profit Tracker are built for people already running something who need a real system. The Business Health Scorecard is useful at any stage.
What if I have a question or need help?
Email us at hello@mysoloos.com. We answer every email — usually within 24 hours on business days.
