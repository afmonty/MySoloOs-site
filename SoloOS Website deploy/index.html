The story

Built by someone who's been in your books.

I spent years managing the books for small businesses before I started running my own. The same problem showed up everywhere: most owners had no real system. Receipts in a folder, a spreadsheet that stopped making sense in March, and no clear picture of whether they were actually making money.

I built SoloOS because I couldn't find anything that worked for real people running real businesses. Not a course. Not complicated accounting software. Just practical systems that give you the full picture without requiring a finance degree to use them.

Every product is designed to turn messy numbers into a clear next decision.

Questions

The ones we get most.

What format are the tools?
The Business Control Center is an interactive HTML system that opens in your browser and auto-saves your work. The Bill & Budget Tracker is a fillable PDF. The Side Hustle Profit Tracker, Debt Payoff Planner, and Savings Challenge Tracker are printable digital PDFs that work with common annotation apps.
How do I get my purchase?
Instant digital download — your file is available immediately after purchase through Gumroad. You'll also receive a download link by email.
Can I use these on my phone?
Yes. The Business Control Center opens in a modern browser, and most PDF apps on iOS and Android support the fillable planners. For the best experience, a computer or tablet is recommended.
Where is my Business Control Center data stored?
Your information stays in your browser. The Business Control Center includes a versioned backup and restore feature so you can protect your work or move it to another device. You can also export income and expenses as a CSV file for your records or accountant.
Are these good for a new business or an established one?
Both. The Savings Challenge and Bill & Budget Tracker are great starting points if you're just getting organized. The Business Control Center and Side Hustle Profit Tracker are built for people already running something who need a real system. The Business Health Scorecard is useful at any stage.
What if I have a question or need help?
Email us at hello@mysoloos.com. We answer every email — usually within 24 hours on business days.
